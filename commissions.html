Terms of Service


I will always ask if you've read the terms of service, and I consider discord chat messages and emails part of a written agreement. It is possible to discuss and renegotiate those terms.

Pricing Methods


Commissions below $50 will be paid in full after a thumbnail is approved. Commissions exceeding $50 can be paid in half after the sketch, and before the full-res version of the work is sent to you.

Please be absolutely certain that this is the perspective, pose, and tone that you want for your character, I do not mind creating more thumbnails versus restarting after 6 hours of work. Additional charges can occur for restarting large portions of work.

Also, I occasionally upgrade commissions by a tier if I'm having fun. It might be a waste of my time, but this will be of no charge to you. If you definitely don't want this to happen, let me know. I try to ask.

Time & Delays


The artwork can take anywhere from 3 hours (including consultation) to 2 months to complete.

If you have deadlines to consider, please be clear on this from the beginning. I will let you know if your piece is possible to complete in such a time frame.

Small corrections are entirely free of charge.

If there is a miscommunication in our consultation that results in a large, unexpected correction that adds 1 hour or more of work, there will be an additional charge.

You will not be charged for my forgetfulness. You will not be charged for small corrections that eventually accumulate to an hour of work.

Termination


I do have the right to refund your money at any point and cease working on your commission. You will be fully refunded in this case, minus the tax removed from PayPal.
